A little bit about us

We work with professional artists and designers to create a high-end collection of temporary tattoos we're proud of.

With every Tattly you buy, we give back a generous royalty to the artist who designed it.

In 2017, we hit $1 million in total payouts to our artists. That makes us really happy!
